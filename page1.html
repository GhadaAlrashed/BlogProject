Connect with Microsoft Remotely at PyCon 2021!

Luciana
Posted on: May 6, 2021 at 10:24 AM
13 likes 3 comments
#Python

PyCon 2021 is almost here, and we’re excited to support this incredible event once again! We’ve been connecting with the community at PyCon in person from 2017 to 2019, and then remotely in 2020. Even though we’re sad we still can’t meet you in person in 2021, with this remote edition we will have the opportunity to engage with folks from all over the world through the exciting activities we are bringing this year – and that keeps us going. If you’re curious to learn more about what we are doing at PyCon and how you can connect with us, keep on reading below!

Virtual booth

In our virtual booth, we will be showing recorded demos featuring many of the products and services Microsoft offers for Python development. You will see Visual Studio Code and Notebooks, Postgres in the cloud, Azure Machine Learning, Azure SDKs and more! We will also be live in our lounge demo booth showing some demos and answering any questions you may have.

Workshop: Deploying your Django application for scale with Microsoft Azure

You can also join us at our workshop so you can learn about scalable Django architecture and how Azure services like load balancing, sharded databases, and functions can be used to scale a Django application!
